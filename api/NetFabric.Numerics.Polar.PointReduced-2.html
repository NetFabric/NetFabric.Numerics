Remarks

In a Polar coordinate system, a reduced point is represented by a pair of values: the radius and the azimuth. The radius represents the distance from the origin, and the azimuth represents the angle measured counterclockwise from a reference direction within a full revolution. The choice of angle units is determined by the specified angle units type, TAngleUnits.
